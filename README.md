# Magsafe-based-camera-lens-accessory-system
Magsafe based camera lens accessory system.

Most camera lens accessorts have threads meant for "easy removal" But to me they take along time to setup and isn't that easy to use in a quick pinch.
Most of the 3D print files are made for the Sony E mount lens. Or if other lens has extra spaces on the outer rim.

Alls parts are bought off sellers. The standerds and size might differ, so do double check before buying. (Magsafe is mostly a universal standard so most adapters should be compataible.)

Get a standerd magsafe metal ring. And Magsafe wall mount adapter(The side with magnsafe inside)

Stick the metal ring to the top of the lens, then stick the magnet to the accessory you want magnetized
